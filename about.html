About Us

In 1967, Mr Suresh Shah, the founder of Sapna-Group came to Bangalore from Mumbai and set up a 10 X 10 sq feet of a bookshop, the humble beginning of Sapna. Today Sapna has 16+ retail stores. Sapna has expanded beyond its Retail Business to encompass Publishing, Pan-India Distribution, Ecommerce, Manufacturing, Technology, University Supplies,School Connect Programmes as well as Corporate Distribution . Embrace the Journey for this is no ordinary tale. This is a tale of tremendous hardship with a vision to impact the world.
